Decaf

A quiet place to look at paintings.

Over 23,000 public domain works from the world's greatest museums. Yours to explore, free from noise.

Download on the App Store

Decaf is a scrolling feed of public domain paintings from nine of the world's great museums, including the Metropolitan Museum of Art, the Rijksmuseum, the Art Institute of Chicago, and the Smithsonian Institution. These institutions have made their collections freely available through open access programs, and Decaf brings them together in a single, unhurried feed.

There is no algorithm deciding what you see. No advertisements. No record of what you pause on or pass by. Paintings appear in a different order every time, drawn from over 23,000 works spanning centuries and continents.

No subscription. No tricks.
