Privacy Policy

Decaf is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data Collection

Decaf collects no personal data. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, and no registration required to use the app.

Network Requests

Decaf fetches painting images and metadata directly from museum APIs over HTTPS. These requests are made to the servers of the Metropolitan Museum of Art, Rijksmuseum, Art Institute of Chicago, National Gallery of Art, Smithsonian Institution, Cleveland Museum of Art, Walters Art Museum, and Belvedere Vienna. Decaf has no servers of its own.

Local Storage

Saved paintings and a record of recently viewed painting IDs are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

Analytics and Advertising

Decaf contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Third Parties

Decaf does not share any information with third parties because no information is collected in the first place.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy please contact us at hello@artdecaf.app.
